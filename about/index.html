Who We Are

The Baarik Difference

Holidays by Baarik started with a straightforward idea: help Canadian families plan holidays worth having, without the stress, without the guesswork, and without settling for generic packages. Based in Mississauga, we serve clients across the GTA and across Canada.

We plan everything from intimate getaways to destination weddings for hundreds of guests. Halal-friendly options are available across our packages for families who need them. Every trip we plan reflects the care our team puts into the details.
