Eid Travel

Eid Travel Packages — Celebrate the Holidays Abroad

Eid is the perfect time to mark a new chapter with a family holiday. Holidays by Baarik arranges Eid travel packages for families across Toronto, Mississauga, and the GTA — curating destinations where you can celebrate comfortably without compromise.

Why Travel for Eid?

The Eid break — whether Eid ul-Fitr at the end of Ramadan or Eid ul-Adha — is one of the most meaningful times of the year for Muslim families. Many families use this time to gather, relax, and reward themselves after a period of reflection and worship. Travelling for Eid has become increasingly popular among Canadian Muslim families who want to celebrate in style while experiencing new parts of the world.

Baarik specializes in making Eid travel seamless. We understand the timing requirements, the need for halal dining options at your destination, and the value of having a travel consultant who genuinely understands the holiday's significance.

Best Destinations for Eid Travel

Dubai for Eid ul-Fitr

Dubai is one of the most popular Eid destinations for Canadian Muslims — and for good reason. The city comes alive during Eid ul-Fitr with extended shopping hours, spectacular fireworks, special dining events, and festive decorations throughout the malls and waterfront. Hotels offer special Eid buffets and family packages. Our Dubai packages start from $2,800 per person.

Turkey for Eid ul-Adha

Eid ul-Adha, also known as Kurban Bayramı in Turkey, is deeply celebrated across the country. Visiting Turkey during Eid ul-Adha means experiencing the holiday as a genuine cultural event — not just as a tourist. From Istanbul's grand mosques to Cappadocia's magical landscape, Turkey offers a profound connection to Islamic heritage alongside world-class hospitality. Our Turkey packages start from $2,400 per person.

The Maldives — A Private Eid Escape

For families who prefer a quiet, private Eid celebration away from the crowds, the Maldives is an exceptional choice. Overwater villas offer complete privacy, the islands are predominantly Muslim, and the natural beauty creates a sense of gratitude that feels especially fitting during the holidays. Maldives Eid packages from $4,200 per person. See Maldives packages.

Morocco — Eid at the Heart of Islamic Culture

Morocco celebrates Eid with great ceremony, particularly Eid ul-Adha. The streets of Marrakech, Fez, and Casablanca come alive with the sights, sounds, and aromas of the holiday. Staying in a traditional riad during Eid offers an immersive cultural experience unlike any other destination. Our Morocco packages start from $2,200 per person.

Planning Your Eid Trip: What to Know

Book Early — Eid Dates Are Competitive

Eid ul-Fitr and Eid ul-Adha are peak travel periods for Muslim travellers worldwide. Flights and premium accommodations sell out quickly, especially during Eid ul-Fitr when many families have an extended break. We recommend booking your Eid travel at least three to four months in advance.

Eid Dates Vary Each Year

Because Eid is determined by the Islamic lunar calendar, exact dates shift each year. Our team stays up to date on projected Eid dates and can plan your trip around them, including flexible booking options in case dates shift by a day.

Family-Sized Accommodations

Eid travel is typically family travel. We prioritize accommodations with family suites, connecting rooms, and spacious villas rather than standard hotel rooms. We also arrange family-friendly dining and activities that work for guests of all ages.
