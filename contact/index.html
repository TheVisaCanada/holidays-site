Frequently Asked Questions

What is TICO registration and why does it matter?

TICO (Travel Industry Council of Ontario) registration means we are a licensed travel agency operating under Ontario law. Your bookings are protected, and you have a 10-day free-look period for cancellation and full refund.

Do you only serve Muslim clients?

We welcome clients of all backgrounds. Halal-friendly options are available across our packages for families who need them — but you don't need to require halal dining to work with us.

What destinations do you offer?

We curate experiences worldwide, with particular expertise in the Maldives, Dubai, Turkey, Switzerland, and the Mediterranean. Bespoke itineraries can take you anywhere.

How far in advance should I book?

For leisure travel, we recommend 3-6 months. For destination weddings, 12-18 months is ideal to secure the best venues and rates.

What is included in your packages?

Our packages typically include flights, accommodations, transfers, and dedicated concierge service. Halal-certified dining and prayer facilities are available on many packages. Each package is customised to your needs.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options for most packages. Contact us to discuss a payment schedule that works for you.
